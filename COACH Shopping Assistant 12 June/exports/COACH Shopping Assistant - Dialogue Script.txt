COACH SHOPPING ASSISTANT — CONVERSATION SCRIPT
Mobile web + desktop · UAE · Generated for team review

SPEAKERS
  Customer               — the shopper
  Assistant              — the COACH Shopping Assistant (the bot)
  Layla (Customer Care)  — human agent, after handover
  System                 — UI states (intro cards, offline notice)
Handover points to Customer Care are marked with  → HANDOVER
========================================================================

■ WELCOME
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  [Open]
  Assistant: Hello, I’m your Coach shopping assistant. How can I help today?
     ⤷ Options: Find your next bag (hero) · Find the perfect gift · Payments & BNPL · Delivery & returns · Speak to Customer Care

  [Intro (shown every open)]
  System: Swipeable value-prop cards: “Find your next bag” · “Find the perfect gift” · “Here to help”.
     ⤷ Options: Skip · Next · Get started

■ PRODUCT DISCOVERY
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  [Start]
  Customer: Find your next bag

  [Colour]
  Assistant: I’m here to help you find something special. Do you have a colour in mind?
     ⤷ Options: Black · Brown · Rust · Burgundy (highlighter swatches)

  [Style]
  Assistant: Beautiful choice. Are you looking for a particular style?
     ⤷ Options: Shoulder · Crossbody · Top handle · Surprise me

  [Budget]
  Assistant: Do you have a price range in mind?
     ⤷ Options: Under AED 2,000 · AED 2,000–2,500 · No limit

  [Results]
  Assistant: Here’s what we’d suggest. Take a look, add to bag or tap for details.
     ⤷ Options: [Product carousel] Refine my search · Show gifts instead · Talk to a stylist

  [Talk to a stylist]   → HANDOVER TO CUSTOMER CARE
  Assistant: (connects to Customer Care)

  [Add to bag]
  Assistant: ✓ Added to your bag — {product}, {colour} · {price}.
     ⤷ Options: View bag

■ GIFTING
------------------------------------------------------------------------

     ⤷ Options: My partner · A friend · My mum · Treat myself

■ PAYMENTS & BNPL (INFORMATIONAL)
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  [Start]
  Customer: Payments & BNPL

  [Menu]
  Assistant: Of course, how can I help?
     ⤷ Options: How Tabby works · How Tamara works · Payment methods · Speak to Customer Care

  [How Tabby works]
  Assistant: Tabby lets you split the total into 4 interest-free payments. [Shows 4-instalment breakdown]
     ⤷ Options: How Tamara works · Payment methods · Speak to Customer Care

  [How Tamara works]
  Assistant: Tamara lets you split the total into 4 interest-free payments. [Shows 4-instalment breakdown]
     ⤷ Options: How Tabby works · Payment methods · Speak to Customer Care

  Assistant: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Apple Pay and Cash on Delivery. Tabby and Tamara are also available at checkout for interest-free instalments.
     ⤷ Options: How Tabby works · Speak to Customer Care · Back to menu

  [Speak to Customer Care]   → HANDOVER TO CUSTOMER CARE
  Assistant: (connects to Customer Care)

■ CUSTOMER CARE (DEDICATED HANDOVER)
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  [Entry — explicit]
  Customer: I want to speak to the Customer Care team
     ⤷ Options: (welcome card · ≡ menu · Payments option)

  [Connect line]   → HANDOVER TO CUSTOMER CARE
  Assistant: Of course! Let me connect you with our Customer Care team right away. 🙋

  [Entry — upset (free text)]
  Customer: e.g. “I am so angry about my order”

  [Empathetic connect]   → HANDOVER TO CUSTOMER CARE
  Assistant: I’m sorry to hear that. Let me connect you with our Customer Care team right away.

  [Within hours — connecting]   → HANDOVER TO CUSTOMER CARE
  Assistant: [Agent card: Connecting…]

  [Within hours — live agent]   → HANDOVER TO CUSTOMER CARE
  Layla (Customer Care): Hello, I’m Layla from Coach Customer Care. How can I help you today?
     ⤷ Options: Thanks, Layla

  [Outside hours — offline]   → HANDOVER TO CUSTOMER CARE
  System: Thanks for contacting Coach. Our Customer Care team is currently offline. We’re available every day from 10am to 10pm UAE time. Please email us and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible. Speak soon.
     ⤷ Options: Close chat · Save transcript

  [Outside hours — transcript]
  Assistant: Your transcript has been saved and emailed to you. We’ll be in touch soon.
